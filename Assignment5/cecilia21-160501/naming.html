Naming Strategy

  • Domain: http://example.linkeddata.es/
  • Ontological terms path: http://example.linkeddata.es/my_vocabulary#
  • Individual path: http://example.linkeddata.es/instances/
  • Ontological terms pattern: http://example.linkeddata.es/vocab/vocab#term
  • Individual pattern: http://example.linkeddata.es/instances/<resource_type>/<instance>
